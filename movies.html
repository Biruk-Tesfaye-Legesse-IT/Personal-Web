Movies

I am big fan of series movies. Especially situational comedy and crime genres. Here are some of my all-time favourites from those I've watched over the years I think many would enjoy!

Friends

Year: 1994-2004

Genre: Sitcom

Follow the lives of six reckless adults living in Manhattan, as they indulge in adventures which make their lives both troublesome and happening.

The Big Bang Theory

Year: 2007-2019

Genre: Sitcom

Brooklyn Nine-Nine
